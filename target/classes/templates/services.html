Our Services

Basic Package

A 45-minute virtual meeting to discuss your academic background, English proficiency and the application process

  • A 45-minute virtual meeting
  • Assessment of academic background and interests
  • Overview of application requirements
  • Determining eligibility for studying in the Abroad
  • Breakdown of costs and expenses
  • Briefing about the Abroad education system

Standard Package

Application support up to 10 universities, visa support, TOEFL and GRE/GMAT assistance

  • Application support up to 12 universities
  • Feedback on the Statement of Purpose with regard to grammar and coherence
  • Review of application documents for each school/program
  • Resources for TOEFL/IELTS/GRE/GMAT
  • Visa Guidance
  • Support for official communication with target schools

Full Package

Standard Package + application submission, portfolio review, and loan/scholarship opportunities

  • Everything included in the Standard Package
  • Managing the students' application portal
  • Ensuring the timely submission of all application documents within the specified application deadline
  • Exploring extensive no-collateral/no-cosigner student loans and financial aid opportunities abroad
