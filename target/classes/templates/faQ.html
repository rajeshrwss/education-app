Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is admission to the schools applied for guaranteed?
While we are highly confident in the quality of our services and the work we do, we cannot guarantee admission to any school due to the many factors involved in the application process.
2. I'm unsure if my English proficiency is sufficient to apply to a school in the US. Could you help me with this?
Absolutely! Our team includes experienced English language teachers with Master's degrees and PhDs. We will assess your English proficiency together and inform you if it's sufficient for your application.
3. I’m a bit unclear about the service packages you offer. How can I contact you to get more details?
You can contact us through the form on our website or by emailing us.
4. I’ve reviewed your packages and I’m ready to get started. What’s the next step?
Great! The next step is to complete the application form on our website. We will get back to you promptly and arrange an online pre-assessment session.
5. I’ve never been abroad before and I'm unsure if I can adjust to living in a foreign country. Can you assist me with that?
Being a Professional team, we are ready to answer all your questions from academic life to daily life abroad.
6. I’m not familiar with the TOEFL and/or GRE exams. Could you provide me with more information about them?
Yes, We provide all the necessary information on the TOEFL/IELTS/GRE exams, we also help you with the preparation process
7. My undergraduate degree is in a different field from the one I want to pursue in graduate school. Am I still eligible to apply?
Absolutely! In this case, you will need to provide a detailed explanation of your motivation in your statement of purpose and complete any required prerequisite courses.
8. Are there any full-ride scholarships available for international students?
Full-ride scholarships for Master's programs are quite limited. However, securing a full-ride scholarship for PhD programs is more common. Additionally, there are external scholarship programs, like the Fulbright Scholarship, that can cover all your educational expenses.
9. Are there any age limitations for graduate programs?
No, there are no age restrictions for graduate programs. Students of all ages are encouraged to apply.
10. Will I be allowed to work while studying in the US?
You are allowed to work during term time for a maximum of 20 hours per week. After your first year of study, you may be eligible to work off-campus if you can demonstrate financial need (fluctuations in USD/TRY parity are considered a valid reason for requesting off-campus work authorization). Additionally, you can participate in internships through Curricular Practical Training (CPT) and work either full-time or part-time through Optional Practical Training (OPT) during and after your studies. Please contact us for more details.
11. Do you offer visa consulting services for students?
Yes, we offer visa consulting services for students. Our services include assisting with document preparation, filling out the DS-160 form, creating the SEVIS record, scheduling the visa interview, and providing interview practice.
